# 1. City

The city is abstracted as a **discretized ruler** of $n_{\text{cells}}$ parcels with uniform width $\Delta x = L / n_{\text{cells}}$.

$$
\text{position}(i) = (i + 0.5) \cdot \Delta x
$$

The **CBD** sits at $i_{\text{CBD}} = \lfloor n_{\text{cells}} / 2 \rfloor$. All trips target this cell.

## Population

Each cell has $\rho$ households split across 3 socioeconomic strata:

| Stratum | Default share | Income |
|---------|--------------:|--------:|
| High (1) | 10 % | $120$ |
| Middle (2) | 40 % | $50$  |
| Low (3)  | 50 % | $10$  |

Each agent draws attributes: `remote_work` with probability $P^s_{\text{remote}}$, `has_car` with probability $P^s_{\text{car}}$.

<Callout type="warn">
**Strong assumption**: every worker leaves their origin cell to the CBD and returns — exclusive Home–Work–Home tours. No trip chaining.
</Callout>
